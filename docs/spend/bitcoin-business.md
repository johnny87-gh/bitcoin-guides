- Understand legal requirements
- Consult professionals
- Develop strategy
- Stakeholder buy-in

### Phase 2: Setup (Weeks 5-8)
- Choose Bitcoin wallet
- Set up payment processing
- Configure accounting integration
- Develop policies and procedures
- Train staff

### Phase 3: Testing (Weeks 9-10)
- Test with small amounts
- Verify all integrations
- Test customer experience
- Document issues
- Refine processes

### Phase 4: Launch (Week 11-12)
- Soft launch with select customers
- Monitor performance

- Adjust processes
- Full launch

## Summary

Bitcoin can be integrated into business operations with proper planning, tools, and professional advice. Benefits include lower fees, access to global markets, and competitive advantage. Challenges include volatility, regulation, and technical complexity.

**Recommendation**: Start small, test thoroughly, consult professionals, and scale gradually. Understand the benefits and risks before full adoption.
