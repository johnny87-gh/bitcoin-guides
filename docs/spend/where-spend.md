## Satsback Rewards

### What are Satsback Rewards?

Earn Bitcoin (satoshis) back when you shop with partner retailers:
- Similar to cashback or rewards programs
- Paid in Bitcoin instead of fiat
- Participating retailers pay in Bitcoin
- Accumulate Bitcoin over time
- Easy way to stack sats

### Satsback Platforms

#### Lolli
- Browser extension and mobile app
- Large partner network
- Up to 30% back in Bitcoin
- Easy to use
- Referral program
- US-focused

#### Fold App
- Bitcoin rewards and payments
- Lightning Network integration
- Spin to earn more sats
- Major retailer partners
- Cash App integration
- Mobile app

#### BitcoinRewards
- Cashback in Bitcoin
- Extensive retailer network
- Easy sign-up
- Instant rewards
- Mobile-friendly
- Global availability

#### Fold Rewards
- Spin wheel for extra sats
- Supercharge rewards
- Bitcoin back on purchases
- Major retailers
- Lightning integration
- Gamified experience

#### Satsback (by various providers)
- Various specialized programs
- Category-specific rewards
- Mobile apps available
- Community-driven
- Different reward structures

### How Satsback Works
1. Create account on rewards platform
2. Shop through their links or browser extension
2. Purchase as normal at partner retailers
3. Platform tracks purchase
4. Receive sats back to your wallet
5. Accumulate Bitcoin over time

### Tips for Maximizing Satsback
- Install browser extensions
- Check before every online purchase
- Compare rewards across platforms
- Refer friends for bonus sats
- Use Lightning for faster payouts
- Combine with credit card rewards
