# Financial Services

Access loans, earn interest, and manage taxes with Bitcoin-based financial services.

## Bitcoin Lending and Borrowing

### What is Bitcoin Lending?

Bitcoin lending allows you to:
- Borrow against Bitcoin holdings
- Earn interest on Bitcoin deposits
- Access liquidity without selling
- Leverage Bitcoin holdings
- Generate passive income

### Borrowing Against Bitcoin

#### Why Borrow Against Bitcoin?
- Avoid selling Bitcoin
- Access USD or other fiat
- Potential tax benefits (consult tax professional)
- Maintain Bitcoin exposure
- Leverage holdings without capital gains

#### Loan Terms and Conditions

##### Loan-to-Value (LTV)
- Percentage of Bitcoin value you can borrow
- Higher LTV = more borrowing capacity
- Higher LTV = higher risk
- Margin calls if Bitcoin price drops
- Typical LTV: 20-50%

##### Interest Rates
- Variable based on market conditions
- Competitive vs traditional loans
- Often lower than personal loans
- Rates vary by platform
- Consider total cost of borrowing

##### Loan Terms
- Flexible repayment options
- Interest-only options available
- No fixed term often available
- Prepayment options
- Automatic liquidation if collateral value drops

##### Collateral Requirements
- Bitcoin sent to cold storage
- Insured on some platforms
- Can't use collateral during loan
- Returned upon loan repayment
- Professional custody

### Borrowing Risks and Considerations

#### Price Volatility Risk
- Margin call risk
- Forced liquidation risk
- Bitcoin price can drop significantly
- May require additional collateral
- Understand worst-case scenarios

#### Platform Risk
- Insolvency risk
- Hacking risk

#### Tax Implications
- Interest payments may be tax-deductible
- Consult tax professional
- Understand jurisdiction
- Different tax treatments
- Documentation requirements

#### Counterparty Risk
- Platform solvency
- Regulatory compliance
- Insurance coverage
- Due diligence required
- Diversify platforms

### Tax Mistakes

## Summary

Bitcoin financial services offer loans, interest accounts, and tax tools. Borrow against Bitcoin without selling. Earn interest on deposits. Use tax software for reporting. Understand risks and do due diligence before using any financial service.

**Recommendation**: Start small, understand all terms and risks, and diversify platforms. Never put more Bitcoin in these services than you're willing to lose. Consult tax professionals for tax implications.
