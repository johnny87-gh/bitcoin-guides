# Merchant Tools

Accept Bitcoin payments in your business with these powerful tools and solutions.

## Payment Processing Options

### Self-Hosted vs Third-Party

#### Self-Hosted Solutions
- **BTCPay Server**: Full control, no fees
- **LNBits**: Lightning Network focus
- **SatSale**: Simple Bitcoin sales
- **Casa Node**: Simple setup

**Advantages**: Keep 100% of Bitcoin, no fees, no intermediaries, control

**Disadvantages**: Technical setup required, ongoing maintenance, responsibility for updates

#### Third-Party Processors
- **BitPay**: Major processor, instant fiat conversion
- **CoinGate**: Multi-cryptocurrency, good rates
- **CoinPayments**: Wide coin support, shopping cart plugins
- **OpenNode**: Lightning and on-chain, simple integration
- **NowPayments**: Multi-cryptocurrency, low fees

**Advantages**: Easy setup, instant fiat conversion, customer support, less technical

**Disadvantages**: Fees, loss of Bitcoin control, intermediaries, potential regulatory issues

## Summary

Accepting Bitcoin is easier than ever with modern merchant tools. BTCPay Server offers full control and no fees. LNBits focuses on Lightning Network. Third-party processors like BitPay offer simplicity. Choose based on your business needs and technical comfort.

**Recommendation**: Start with BTCPay Server for full control and maximum benefits. Use Lightning for instant, low-fee payments. Always keep Bitcoin in your own wallet.
