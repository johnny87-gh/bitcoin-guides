## Summary

Bitcoin media provides news, education, and community connection. Bitcoin Magazine and CoinDesk for news. What Bitcoin Did and Unchained for podcasts. Andreas Antonopoulos for education. Diverse sources and fact-checking are important.

**Best Practice**: Consume media from diverse sources. Cross-reference information. Understand different perspectives. Be skeptical of financial advice. Use media to learn, not to make investment decisions.

**Important**: Bitcoin media has various biases and perspectives. No single source is perfect. Critical thinking and fact-checking are essential. Use media to inform, not to make investment decisions without your own research.
